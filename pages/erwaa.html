Erwaa

Where Every Drop Counts

Overview

Irwaa is a smart irrigation project that uses AI to optimize water usage based on real environmental data. It analyzes soil type, plant type, and weather conditions to decide when and how much water each plant needs, ensuring efficient and sustainable irrigation.

The Problem

Traditional irrigation systems rely on fixed schedules without considering actual plant needs or changing weather conditions. This leads to water waste and can negatively affect plant health.

The Goal

To build an intelligent system that makes automated irrigation decisions using real-time environmental data, while also supporting users with weather forecasts, helpful tips, and preventive warnings.
